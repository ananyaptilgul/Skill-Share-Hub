About SkillShareHub

SkillShareHub is a community-driven platform designed to help users discover new hobbies, learn valuable skills, and connect with others. Our mission is to make learning accessible, engaging, and collaborative.

Core Features:

  • Dynamic skill browsing with categories.
  • Personal learning roadmaps and note-taking.
  • A gamified rewards system with points and badges.
  • A community map to find local users.
  • An AI assistant to answer your questions.
